Famous units of the Civil War

The Iron Brigade

The Iron Brigade was a famous unit of the civil war, serving with distinction in multiple major batles. It grew to fame under the command of John Gibbon at the battle of South Mountain. It took the highest percentage of casualties of all the units that fought in the civil war and it served all the way to the end of the war.

Major Battles fought

  1. 2nd Bull Run
  2. Antietam
  3. Fredericksburg
  4. Chancellorsville
  5. Gettysburg
  6. Overland Campaign
  7. Appomattox

The Stonewall Brigade

The stonewall brigade was another famous unit of the cival war, fighting with the Confederacy up untill 1864. It became well known after the First Battle of Bull Run where the Stonewall Brigade under Stonewall Jackson held off the union army in time for reinforcements to arrive. It was dissolved after Spotslvania as after the fighting, only 200 men remained with the commanders killed.

Major Battles fought

  1. 1st Bull Run
  2. Seven Days Battles
  3. 2nd Bull Run
  4. Antietam
  5. Fredericksburg
  6. Chancellorsville
  7. Overland Campaign

The Fighting 69th

The Fighting 69th, also known as the Irish Brigade distinguished itself as a brigade consisting of Irish immigrants. They were known for their use of "buck and ball" which were close range shotguns instead of the standard infantry rifle. The brigade was also known for its aggressiveness and bravery in combat, fighting bravely in many battles.

Major Battles fought

  1. Seven Days Battles
  2. Antietam
  3. Fredericksburg
  4. Chancellorsville
  5. Overland Campaign
  6. Appomattox
